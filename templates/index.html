About This Project

Our Heart Disease Prediction System uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze cardiovascular health indicators and predict the likelihood of heart disease. This tool is designed to assist healthcare professionals and individuals in early detection and prevention of cardiovascular conditions.

The system analyzes 13 key medical parameters including age, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, and various cardiac measurements to provide accurate predictions. Our model has been trained on extensive medical datasets and achieves high accuracy in identifying potential heart disease risks.

Disclaimer: This tool is for informational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice. Always consult with qualified healthcare providers for medical decisions.
