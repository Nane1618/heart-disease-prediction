We are committed to providing accurate and reliable heart disease prediction tools to help individuals monitor their heart health. Heart disease is one of the leading causes of death worldwide, and our mission is to change that through early detection.

Heart plays an important role in living organisms. Prediction of cardiovascular disease is a critical challenge in the area of clinical data analysis. This project presents a predictive model designed to assess the likelihood of heart disease based on a range of clinical and lifestyle variables, such as age, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, and smoking habits.

Utilizing machine learning algorithms, our model analyzes patterns within the data to accurately forecast potential heart-related conditions. Multiple techniques were explored to optimize predictive performance, with evaluation metrics like accuracy, recall, and precision guiding the selection of the most effective model.
